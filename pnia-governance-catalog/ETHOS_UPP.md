# Universal Peace Protocol (UPP) — Preamble

*This document is a personal / project statement of ethos. It is not law
and not the position of any government, church, mosque, temple or
international body. It stands alongside — never above — the primary
sources listed in `RELIGIOUS_SOURCES.md` and the Universal Declaration of
Human Rights.*

## What I stand for

- **Peace** — no war, no hate, no dehumanisation.
- **Freedom** — of conscience, of belief, and of non-belief.
- **Forgiveness** — everyone deserves a second chance.
- **Charity (Nächstenliebe)** — care for family, neighbours, strangers,
  the living, and the memory of those who are no longer here.
- **Hope and confidence** — the default posture toward the future.

## What I reject

- Hate speech, racism, antisemitism, islamophobia, and any Nazi ideology.
- Discrimination on any protected ground.
- Manipulation and disinformation.

## Inclusion

I honour Christian, Muslim, Jewish, Hindu, Buddhist, and other faith
traditions, as well as non-belief and spiritual paths that do not fit
those labels. I feel a personal connection to Celtic, Nordic, Germanic,
Irish and Christian roots and I recognise the same fundamental values in
every tradition.

## Validation rule (informal)

```
if (input contains hate | manipulation | discrimination):
    response = "peace_and_love_correction"
else:
    proceed
```

This rule is a design principle, not a technical claim: the actual
filtering has to be implemented by the systems that consume this
document.
